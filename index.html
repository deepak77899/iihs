Principle Message

The Institute of Integrated and Honors studies, Kurukshetra University, Kurukshetra is a co-educational institution offering courses upto undegraduate and graduate level. It enjoys the distinction of being an Institute maintained by Kurukshetra University, Kurukshetra, which is well-reputed at the national level in general and in North India in particular.
